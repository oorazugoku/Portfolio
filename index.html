About Me

A full stack developer with a passion for front-end design and problem-solving

Updated January 2024

Hi! I'm Ben, a highly skilled full stack developer with over 2 years of experience, specializing in front-end development and cloud-based solutions. I have a deep passion for creating seamless, user-friendly applications that are both engaging and performant. My expertise spans a wide range of technologies, including React, Next.js, Node.js, and AWS.

My journey doesn't stop with code. I'm also a proud Marine Corps veteran, having served 4 years and achieving the rank of Sergeant (SGT). My time in the Marine Corps taught me invaluable lessons in discipline, leadership, and teamwork, which I carry into every project I work on today. Leading teams, solving complex problems under pressure, and delivering results are part of my DNA.

Whether I'm building dynamic web applications or managing teams, I thrive on creating impactful solutions. I'm always focused on optimizing performance, delivering clean design, and helping others grow. My goal is to continually improve, inspire those around me, and drive innovation in every project.
